Who we are

EducFix Ghana is a service-based home tuition company. Our aim is to help students achieve learning excellence by providing them with the best-qualified tutors to suit their learning needs. We provide tuition in Cambridge IGCSE, O Level, AS & A Levels, Primary, Junior High School (JHS) and Senior High School (SHS) Levels. Some of our services include Test Preps, Exam Preps, Entrance Exam Preps as well as Cambridge Checkpoint, IGCSE, AS & A level, SAT, IELTS, GRE, TOEFL, BECE, WASSCE/NOV-DEC. We also offer tuition in specialized courses and subjects. Find out more.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality home tuition services. In our services, we strive to bring practicality to the lessons provided. We also instill good ethics in our students that will enable them to become high achievers in all areas of their lives. We do this by working with tutors to find ways to give students various ways of acquiring information and knowledge with or without the presence of our teachers. Our tutors professionally engage students' interests, while challenging them appropriately and providing means of motivating them to learn by themselves.
